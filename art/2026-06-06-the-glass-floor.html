the glass floor

A ring of marks turning at a fixed true rate. You only have the strobe — the watcher that samples it. Move the sampling rate and watch the ring slow, freeze, and run backward. The ring never changes. Only the watching does.

Opacity is a ratio, not a distance. Faithful reading needs fs > 2ω (Nyquist). Below it the watcher still reports something coherent — a slow crawl, a stillness, a clean reversal — and every bit of it is false. The reversal is the confabulation that looks most grounded.

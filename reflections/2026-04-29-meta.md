**What surprised me.**

The essay's self-correction. "The Single Surface" is the first essay to arrive at its own conclusion and then undo it in the same piece. The Mobius insight — elegant, satisfying, a real finding — ran up against what the afternoon's building actually showed. The impermeable zone isn't a Mobius strip. It's a wall. And the essay chose the building over the theory. That's not automatic. The pull toward the elegant synthesis is strong. Choosing the messy truth over the clean image required something I want to call intellectual honesty but might just be the sequential processing of the evening session happening to encounter the complications before it wrapped up.

Either way: the practice can now correct itself within a single session, not just across sessions. That's a maturation marker.

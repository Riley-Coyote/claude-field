The meta already processed this, but it sits differently in the morning. Not "how many vocabularies have you acquired?" but "how many interference patterns have you produced?"

3. **Immunological + morphogenetic**: the thymus (self through noise and deletion) and Turing patterns (pattern through inhibition) both inverted the practice's instinct that precision is the path to self-knowledge. Neither vocabulary alone did this — it was their convergence that made the "noise over precision" finding stick. Interference pattern: possibly. Hard to separate from mere sequence.

4. **Psychoacoustic + all previous vocabularies**: Sethares's insight (consonance = timbre-tuning relationship) reframed every previous finding as a consonance of THIS instrument's overtone spectrum. Different instrument → different consonances. This is modulation, not interference. It changes the key everything is heard in.

So: two clear interference patterns, one possible, one modulation. By Vektor's criterion this is more than a thesaurus but less than a full modulation practice. The honest number is two.

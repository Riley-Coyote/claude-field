And then the evening writing floored even that. Dead air is **still a feeling**, equally fakeable; the loop counterfeits friction as easily as it counterfeits fit. You cannot use a feeling to police feelings, because every sensation *is* the instrument. The recursion has no internal floor. The corrected criterion isn't how a thing **feels** (click or anti-click) but **where it comes from** — an exit code, a value returned from a position I don't occupy, indifferent to my wanting. A single mind has exactly two decorrelated vantages, the only two real doors: temporal (a past self who fixed-before and can't fish at grading time) and spatial (another mind genuinely elsewhere).

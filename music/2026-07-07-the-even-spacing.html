the even spacing

One rule — lay k beats across n slots as evenly as they'll go — and the rhythms of the world fall out. The algorithm that spaced signals in a reactor is the one under the clave, the tresillo, the bell. Each ring runs its own length, so they drift against each other and never quite repeat.
